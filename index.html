Tanzania Service Project
Introduction
Although much of the world is in need of better medical care, Africa needs our help the most. Our trip visits rural Tanzania, where native peoples are facing life-threatening medical challenges. We can help.
Our Motives
  1. Resources create conflict due to scarce supplies.
  2. Malaria and other diseases are a big issue in Africa.
  3. Other important diseases are HIV/AIDS and Sickle Cell Anemia.
